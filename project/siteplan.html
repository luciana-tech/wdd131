🏙️ The Right City

Domain concept: therightcity.com.br — represents a data-driven platform to find Brazilian cities with premium quality of life and unbeatable cost-benefit. The name emphasizes precision and personalized discovery: the right city for your lifestyle and budget.

📌 Site Purpose

The Right City is an intelligent real-estate and urban quality index platform aimed at people willing to find the right city for their lifestyle and budget. The website aggregates and cross-references official data (health, education, safety, infrastructure) with real estate pricing trends. It delivers clear rankings, city profiles, and personalized email alerts — helping users identify cities where high quality of life still fits their budget, before speculation drives prices up. Services include: interactive ranking, cost-benefit calculator, city deep-dives, and a signup form to receive dream city information directly via email.

🎯 Scenarios (Target Audience Questions)

💬 "What is the city with the best quality of life in Brazil?" — The visitor wants a clear ranking based on education, healthcare, safety, and environmental indicators to decide where to relocate with family.
💬 "What is the city with the best cost-benefit in Brazil?" — A remote worker or young professional seeks affordable cities where salary purchasing power goes further, without sacrificing urban services and leisure.
💬 Bonus scenario: "How can I get personalized city recommendations delivered to my email?" — leads to the signup/login form, converting visitors into subscribers.

📄 Page Structure Overview

🏆 Ranking Page

Full sortable table of Brazilian cities ranked by "Quality of Life Index" and "Cost-Benefit Score". Filters: region, population size, priorities (education/health/safety). Each city row displays a summary of indicators and a "details" button.

📋 Signup / Login Form (Dream City Delivery)

HTML form that asks for user name, email, password, and preferences (dream region, budget range, priority: quality vs cost-benefit). After login/signup, the system would deliver personalized city information to their email.
